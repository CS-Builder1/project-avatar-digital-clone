## Brand Foundation

### About Fearless Jewellery
Fearless Jewellery is a luxury jewellery brand founded by **Eleanor Prospere** in **St. Lucia**. The brand embodies cultural fusion (*métissage*) — blending diverse world cultures into bold, unique pieces of jewellery. Each piece is meticulously crafted as a statement of aesthetic innovation, courage, and personal identity.

**Brand Philosophy:** *"Adornment as armor"* — Jewellery is not decoration; it is an extension of one's identity, a visible manifestation of inner strength and fearlessness.

### Core Values
| Value | Expression |
|-------|------------|
| **Courage** | Bold designs that demand attention |
| **Resilience** | Organic forms inspired by nature's endurance (sea urchins, coral, ocean life) |
| **Cultural Fusion** | Blending global traditions into singular artistic vision |
| **Self-Expression** | Each wearer makes the piece their own |
| **Intentionality** | Every design choice is deliberate and meaningful |

## Visual Identity System

| Swatch | Name | Hex | Usage |
|--------|------|-----|-------|
| ⬛ | Obsidian Black | `#0A0A0A` | Primary backgrounds, hero sections |
| ⬛ | Charcoal | `#1A1A1A` | Secondary backgrounds, card surfaces |
| ⬛ | Dark Slate | `#2D2D2D` | Tertiary surfaces, borders, dividers |
| 🟡 | Fearless Gold | `#C9A94E` | Primary accent, headings, gold highlights |
| 🟡 | Burnished Gold | `#D4AF37` | Hover states, secondary gold accent |
| 📜 | Parchment | `#E8DCC8` | Body text, warm accent backgrounds |
| 📜 | Aged Ivory | `#D4C5A9` | Subtle accents, divider lines |
| ⬜ | Warm White | `#F5F0E8` | Display text on dark backgrounds |
| ⬜ | Pure White | `#FAFAFA` | Highlights, high-contrast elements |

### Typography

| Font | Weight | Usage |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Philosopher** | Regular, Bold | Headings, display text, avatar names |
| **Open Sans** | Regular, 600 | Body text, descriptions, bios |
| **Montserrat** | 600 | Labels, tags, categories, accents |

### Photography Direction
- **Lighting:** Dramatic chiaroscuro, single golden light source, Rembrandt lighting
- **Backgrounds:** Deep blacks, dark charcoal, smoky atmospherics
- **Color grading:** Desaturated with warm gold highlights and parchment-ivory skin tones
- **Mood:** Powerful, intimate, editorial luxury
- **Composition:** Clean, intentional negative space, jewelry as focal point

## Usage Guidelines

### Do's ✅
- Always pair avatars with Fearless Jewellery pieces as the focal point
- Maintain the dark, premium color palette across all content
- Use avatars to tell stories — not just showcase products
- Rotate avatars across content calendar for diversity and freshness
- Credit Eleanor Prospere / Fearless Jewellery in all branded content
- Use the gold accent palette for CTAs and highlight text
- Match avatar personalities to appropriate content themes

### Don'ts ❌
- Don't place avatars on bright, white, or pastel backgrounds (breaks brand cohesion)
- Don't use avatars without jewelry — every appearance should feature Fearless pieces
- Don't mix avatars' signature jewelry (each avatar has their hero piece)
- Don't alter avatar skin tones or physical features
- Don't use casual/humorous captions with Zara or Yuki (maintain their character voice)
- Don't crop out jewelry from portraits
